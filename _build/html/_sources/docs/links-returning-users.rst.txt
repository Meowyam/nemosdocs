======================================
Exploring L4 Futher: Intended Audience
======================================

This section is for users who want to learn more about using L4 and have already installed L4 on their computer.

==============
Page Directory
==============

- Users who have just come from the "First Steps" section should explore the "Examples" provided with L4 and work through the "Tutorials" to get more familiar with L4.

- Users who want to use L4's output in other programs should read through "Exporting L4 to Other Formats".

- Users who want to understand how L4 relates to both Law and Computer Science should read through the material in "Law and Computer Science".

- Users who want a dictionary of terms in L4 should refer to both the "Language Specification" and "Cheatsheet" for more information.
