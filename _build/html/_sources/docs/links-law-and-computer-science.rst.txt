===========================================
Law and Computer Science: Intended Audience
===========================================

This section is tailored towards lawyers and computer scientists, both students and practitioners.

==============
Page Directory
==============

- Users who are new to Computational Law or who want to know how L4 fits into Computational Law should read through "L4 and Computational Law".

- Users familiar with certain computer science concepts would benefit from reading "L4 Approaches to the Law".

- Anyone familiar with flowcharts can read "Legal Flowcharts and Software Engineering" to understand how flowcharts can be made better.

- Users familiar with Logic Programming can read "Law and Logic Programming".

- Users familiar with basic Computer Science terminology like Arrays, Dictionaries, and Boolean Structures will understand "Common Computer Science Terms in L4".
